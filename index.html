How Does It Work?

Vidyayan Summer Of Code (VSoC) is a Vidyayan open source initiative that runs for three months. All enrolled participants in this Summer Of Code will have the unique chance to connect with Mentors and the Organizing Team. the mentors and the organizing team from May 2022 to July 30th 2022.

In this period they will extract any help or can resolve any kind of query or uncertainty that they could be having regarding the projects or it’s implementation. The team and therefore the mentors will endeavour to supply the specified help from their absolute best resources.The mentors will declare the issues of the Open Source Projects.If a participant wants to work on a project, he or she must comment on the problem and submit a pull request, and if the mentor approves the work, the participant will be given the chance to do so.

Participants will be able to claim issues on a first-come, first-served basis. The mentors will be required to keep a scoreboard and provide a biweekly review of their individual mentees since the participants will be continually assessed on all of the projects to which they participate.

Following the event's end on May 30, 2022, the organising team will include the mentors' leaderboards in their final appraisal of the participants, and the results will be announced by August, 2022.

The Full Program Timeline

  • Applications Open January 2nd, 2022
    Open source groups interested in serving as participant/mentors in this year's programme can submit an application.
  • Application Deadline March 31, 2022
    All organisations interested in participating in VSoC 2022 must submit their application by 00:30 on March 31th, 2022. (India Standard Time).
  • Review Applications April 15, 2022
    The submitted applications will be reviewed till April 15th..
  • Shortlisted Applicants April 25, 2022
    Shortlisted Participants will be contacted via email and will get an invitation to the workspace.
  • Project Assignment May, 2022
    Allotment of project to the participants.
  • Bonding Session June 5, 2022
    Participants are matched with a Mentor and given the opportunity to begin preparing their projects and milestones. Students spend a month learning more about the community of their organisation. Students work on their VSoC projects.
  • Coding period ends July 30, 2022
    Mentors examine student code samples to see if they finished their VSoC 2022 project effectively.
  • Results declared. August, 2022
    Students are notified of the pass/fail status of their VSoC 2022 projects.
